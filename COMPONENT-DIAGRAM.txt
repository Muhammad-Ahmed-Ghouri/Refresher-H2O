════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                    EXECUTION FLOW (User Journey)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. User visits "/"
   ↓
2. App.js renders MainLayout with Home as Outlet
   ↓
3. MainLayout renders: Navbar → Home → Footer
   ↓
4. Home renders: HomeBanner + Products
   ↓
5. Products displays 5 product cards
   ↓
6. User clicks "Order Now" on a product
   ↓
7. Products.js calls navigate('/contact?id=3')
   ↓
8. App.js renders MainLayout with Contact as Outlet
   ↓
9. MainLayout renders: Navbar → Contact → Footer
   ↓
10. Contact renders: ContactBanner + ContactForm
    ↓
11. ContactForm reads ?id=3 from URL
    ↓
12. ContactForm finds product with id=3
    ↓
13. ContactForm displays product info and form
    ↓
14. User fills form fields
    ↓
15. ContactForm validates in real-time
    ↓
16. Submit button becomes enabled
    ↓
17. User clicks submit
    ↓
18. ContactForm posts to API
    ↓
19. SweetAlert2 shows success message
    ↓
20. Navigate back to "/"
